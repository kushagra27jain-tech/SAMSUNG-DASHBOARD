# 📊 Samsung Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

The Samsung Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end business intelligence platform built in Power BI that gives an overview of the operations within the supply chain. The tool helps monitor supplier performance, manufacturing efficiency, shipment execution, and performance in relation to customers through dynamic visualizations driven by data.

The objective of this project is to create meaningful insight from operational data.


## Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

* Monitor supply chain performance across multiple business functions.
* Track key operational and financial KPIs.
* Analyze supplier and manufacturing effectiveness.
* Evaluate shipment and delivery performance.
* Understand customer behavior and business outcomes.
* Support executive-level decision-making through real-time insights.



## Dashboard Modules

### 🏠 Home

Provides a centralized navigation interface for seamless access to all dashboard sections.

### 📈 Executive Overview

Offers a high-level summary of critical business metrics, trends, and performance indicators.

### 🏭 Supplier Analytics

Analyzes supplier contributions, procurement performance, and supply reliability.

### ⚙️ Manufacturing Analytics

Tracks production efficiency, operational output, and manufacturing performance.

### 🚚 Shipment Analytics

Monitors delivery status, logistics performance, and shipment-related KPIs.

### 👥 Customer Analytics

Provides insights into customer trends, order behavior, and business impact.
